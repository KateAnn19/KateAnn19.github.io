About

Special Education Teacher Turned Web Developer

Before starting web development I was a behavior instructor/teacher for 7+ years with kids and adults with disabilities. I loved teaching and helping kids, but I wanted to explore a different side to my self and stretch myself in new ways. I always loved creating and building things so web development seemed like it could be a good fit. After exploring it for several months to make sure it was the next step in my journey, I fell in love with it and realized it's a natural fit and a field that I feel passionate about.
