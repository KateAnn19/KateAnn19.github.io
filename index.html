Marketing Website

I worked with a team during Lambda School Build Week I. Our assignment was to build an application for Gigapet which is an application that creates avatar pets for kids and kids can feed them by feeding themselves. My part was the marketing website. I added react router, two custom hooks, a search bar, styling and video as additional features to this project.
